Ghana Jollof Rice

A favorite dish made with rice, tomatoes, and a blend of spices, Ghana Jollof Rice is famous for its rich flavor, vibrant color, and the irresistible aroma that fills the kitchen. Often served at parties, weddings, and family gatherings

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 6

Difficulty Level: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 3 cups long-grain rice
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 4 large fresh tomatoes, blended
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium bell pepper, blended
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. onions, garlic, and blended bell pepper until fragrant.
  3. Add the tomato paste and blended tomatoes, cooking until the sauce thickens and oil begins to separate.
  4. Stir in thyme, curry powder, bay leaves, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add chicken stock and bring to a gentle boil.
  6. Rinse rice and add it to the pot, stirring well to coat with the sauce.
  7. Reduce heat, cover, and let cook until rice is tender, adding small amounts of water if needed.
  8. Fluff with a fork and serve hot with fried plantain or grilled chicken.

Tips

For the best flavor, use homemade chicken stock and allow the sauce to cook slowly before adding the rice. Do not over-stir the rice while cooking to avoid it becoming mushy.
